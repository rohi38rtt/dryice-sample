Departing

Check-in online from the comfort of your home or office, with or without baggage anytime before the specified check-in deadline for your flight. You can also choose where you would like to sit (from available seats), download and print your boarding pass or web travel document or elect to receive your boarding pass via SMS (selected flights only) so that you’re ready to go! Web check-in is available for:

  • Domestic Flights:For all Australian, New Zealand and Japan domestic flights - Available between 48 hours and 1 hour before your flight's scheduled departure.
  • International Flights - Available between 48 hours and 2 hours before your flight's scheduled departure.
  • Not available for bookings that include infants or special service requests (such as passengers who require wheelchair assistance, are visually or hearing impaired or who have purchased an extra seat).
  • Not available for staff travellers.
